A Trip to Europe

This past Summer, my girlfriend and I lived in Rome for six week while taking part in Temple University's Study Abroad program. We visited various places in Italy besides Rome such as Florence, Venice, Todi, and The Amalfi Coast. We also took advantage of our geographical location and flew out of Rome to Amsterdam for a weekend trip. Overall, this experience is one I will never forget. Getting immersed in an entirely different culture was eye-opening and something I am grateful to have had the chance to experience.

Amsterdam

The city of Amsterdam is an amazing place. From the beautiful architecture, to the delicious pancakes that are more like crepes, to the Red Light District, it has it all. We only had the opportunity to spend a day and a half here, and I feel like we barely scratched the surface of all there is to do.

Rome

Rome was home base for us this trip. We were fully immersed in the Roman culture as we didn't live too close to the touristy parts of the city. The city was entrancing, the food was incredible, and the coffee , especially the cappucino and ginsend, was the best I've had. I hope to one day return.

Venice

This was my girlfriend's favorite place in Italy. The winding rivers throughout the city are like nothing I've ever seen before. The overall atmosphere of Venice was whimsical. We only had the chance to spend one day here so I hope I can return some day and get the full Venetian experience.
